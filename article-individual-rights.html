Defending Individual Rights

Safeguarding liberty through the courts, legislatures and public debate

Introduction

Individual rights are the bedrock of any free society. Yet governments often infringe on those rights through overreaching laws and enforcement practices. Libertarian organisations respond by litigating, educating and advocating to ensure that due process, proportional punishment and property rights are respected.

Conclusion

The protection of individual rights requires constant vigilance. Through litigation, legislative advocacy and public education, organisations such as CFIF, FAMM and the Mackinac Center remind governments of their constitutional limits.
